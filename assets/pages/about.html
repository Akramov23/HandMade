Who We Are

We are a team of handmade masters passionate about creating useful things with our own hands from materials that everyone can find in their homes. We see handmade craft as our hobby, and are always happy to share it with you.

As a team of enthusiastic handmaders, sewers, and scrapbookers, we also practise in writing articles about various techniques used in handmade art. We also cooperate with suppliers of goods and consumables for handmaders.
